# Exam Timetable — Rules & Schedule

## Golden Rules

1. **Next exam only.** Any given day, ONLY study the subject whose exam comes next.
2. **Early finish = pivot forward.** If Forecasting core is done early, pivot to Causal pre-work. Never idle.
3. **10 marks → 1.5h baseline.** Units with derivations/numericals get extra time.
4. **Pre-exam morning (6-8 AM)** is warm-up only, NOT counted in study time.
5. **Pre-exam bedtime: 12 AM sharp.** No exceptions.
6. **Max 3h continuous study.** Break after every block.
7. **Midnight preferred.** Schedule heavy blocks at night.
8. **Exam day post-exam: study starts at 4 PM.** Rest until then.
